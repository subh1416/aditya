Our Vision

The Way Forward

As a democracy, India deserves better. Citizens have waited too long for a system that works with them rather than against them. In recent years, there has been a visible rise in public frustration, with people increasingly using digital platforms to demand answers from the government.

These voices, however, remain scattered and unorganized. Praja Setu seeks to provide a channel for this growing energy to transform frustration into participation, opacity into accountability, and passive citizenship into active democracy.

Accountability

While political and fiscal empowerment of Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) requires significant structural reforms, the process can begin from the ground up. Empowering citizens is the first step.

We propose the establishment of a civic-tech startup that acts as both an enabler and a watchdog. The objective is to redesign governance through data insights and community building, ensuring accountability at every stage of service delivery.

Responsible Department

Water Supply Board (WSB)

Legal Timeline (SLA)

14 Working Days

Mandatory Fee (500 Ltr/day)

₹ 3,500 (Non-refundable)

1.

Application Method:

Online: Mandatory via WSB Portal (Link: `wsb.gov.in/apply`). **No physical form accepted.**

2.

Required Documents Checklist:

  • Proof of Ownership (Sale Deed/Tax Receipt)
  • Aadhaar Card of Applicant
  • Plumber's Installation Certificate
3.

Physical Visit (If Required):

Visit the **WSB Zonal Office - North Block** (Contact: Mr. Sharma, 98765-XXXXX) for final inspection scheduling **only after** online application approval (Status Code: `AP-4`).
